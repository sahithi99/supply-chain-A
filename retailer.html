Retailer

The rules of the game are pretty simple. You will play the role of a retailer and the computer will act out systematic roles of the Wholesaler, Distributor, and Production Unit. Thus, the game will start with you.

  • As a retailer, you will first need to assess customer demands and check if you have enough to supply from your inventory

For example, the customer demands 2 beers; you need to make sure that there are 2 or more units in your inventory to match the supply

  • After this, you as the retailer will check the order and make sure that you have the number of units to supply as per your customer order
  • You then deliver the 2 beer cans to the customer or as many as the customer wants
  • Once the delivery is done, you will need to recheck customer demands and make sure that your inventory is stocked. For this, you will have to place an order for another 2 units with the wholesaler once the delivery cycle is completed
