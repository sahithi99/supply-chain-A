Project Description


The students will specifically, develop and test a scaled-down, simplified model of a blockchain that is used to provide traceability within a supply chain. While simple and easy to use, the model should faithfully mimic the properties of a blockchain, in the sense that it should be able to do the major things that a blockchain can do. The model could consist of a simulated, game-like supply chain where four or so players can play, with a blockchain that is shared by all, and is used to track certain properties of the goods flowing in the supply chain.
