Distributor

  • The distributor first checks his inventory that is the achievable target to supply consumers with the desired order of beers
  • The distributor receives orders from the wholesaler on the number of units that are required for the cycle
  • The number of demanded beers is shifted to the wholesaler, and the wholesaler shifts them to the retailer
  • The orders placed by the wholesaler for the next cycles are checked again and the process repeats itself
